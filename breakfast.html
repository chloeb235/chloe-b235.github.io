  • Mix 1 cup all-purpose flour w/ 1 cup cold water + a pinch of salt to form batter

Tamagoyaki ðŸ‡¯ðŸ‡µ

  • Whisk 4 eggs w/ 1 tbsp of sugar, 1 tbsp of soy sauce, and pinch of salt
  • Heat and lightly grease a non-stick skillet
  • Pour a thin egg layer and swirl to cover, cook until set but still wet, then roll towards you
  • Push rolled egg aside, add more oil, pour in another egg layer, lifting the roll to let new mixture flow underneath
  • Repeat until all mixture is used
  • Cool slightly, then slice into pieces

  • Mix 1 cup flour, 1 cup water, and pinch of salt to make smooth batter

  • Crack egg over set better, spread evenly and sprinkle w/ chopped scallions

  • Fold crepe into quarters, serve w/ hoisin sauce + chili paste
